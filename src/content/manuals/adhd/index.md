This manual is not written by a clinician. It is written by someone who spent a long time not knowing they had ADHD, and then spent more time figuring out what to do about it.

It starts where most people actually start: confused, a bit overwhelmed, and not entirely sure the label even applies to them.

If that's you — this is for you.

## How to use this

Read it in order the first time. After that, use it as reference. Come back to sections when they're relevant to where you are.

The "last tended" date at the top tells you when I last updated this. My understanding keeps evolving, and so does this document.
