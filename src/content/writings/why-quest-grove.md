---
title: A note on why this place is called Quest Grove
date: 2025-05-28
tags: [quest-grove, meta]
description: The name is not decorative.
---

A grove is a small stand of trees. Not a forest — a forest is where you can get lost. A grove is where you stop and rest before continuing.

A quest is a journey with a direction but not a fixed destination. You know roughly what you're moving toward. You don't know exactly what you'll find.

Quest Grove is the place you return to between quests. You resupply here. You write down what you learned. You rest. Then you go back out.

That is the whole idea.
